ENTRY THEMES

Your entry can fall under any of these themes - judges will assign the category, just shoot what moves you...

  • 📸 Personal Memories: Self-portraits, personal travel memories, and emotional connections to places.
  • ✨ Unexpected Encounters: Spontaneous candid captures, unplanned interactions, and sudden moments of wonder.
  • ⛰️ Grand Landscapes: Architecture, epic natural wonders, iconic landmarks, and vistas.
  • 🏃‍♂️ Life in Motion: Cultural festivals, action, streets, local movement, and immersive lifestyle.

ELIGIBILITY

  • Open to all smartphone users aged 18 and above.
  • Only JPG/JPEG format images are eligible for upload.
  • Must be a MakeMyTrip user (domestic and international travelers welcome).
  • Not eligible in: Germany, United States, Sudan, North Korea, Syria, Iran, Cuba, or the Crimea region.
  • Employees of OPPO, OnePlus, realme, and MakeMyTrip are not eligible to win.
