ELIGIBILITY

  • Images must feature wide-angle, horizontal compositions capturing natural environments, architectural vistas, or cityscapes with high-resolution clarity.
  • Submissions should capture candid or posed group moments within a travel or tour setting, ensuring all individuals are clearly visible and the setting is identifiable.
  • Open to operators aged 18 and above.
  • Only PNG/JPG/JPEG format images are eligible for upload.
  • All submissions must be captured on an OPPO device using a native OPPO filter and must feature either a landscape vista or a group tour setting.
  • Not eligible in: Germany, United States, Sudan, North Korea, Syria, Iran, Cuba, or the Crimea region.
  • Participation is exclusively restricted to official Tour Managers currently affiliated with MakeMyTrip or Flamingo.
